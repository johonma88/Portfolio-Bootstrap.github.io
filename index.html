About Me


Hola! My name is Johanna Honma. I was born in Lima, Peru. My grandparents were from Japan, they immigrated to Peru after the WWII. Lima is located in South America by the Pacific Ocean. Peru is best known for Macchu Picchu, the delicious food and warm nice people. It's been almost 10 years since I moved to the United States.



I have two sons, Toshi and Yoshi (10 and 1 years old) and a dog (Charlie). I love Colorado, I love being outside hiking, camping, fishing, having a picnic . . .

I have a computer science degree from San Martin de Porres University. (Lima, Peru) I'm currently enroll in the Web Developer Boot Camp at University of Denver. to become a Full Stack Web Developer
